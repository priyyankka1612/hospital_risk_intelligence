🏥 **Hospital Operations & Patient Risk Intelligence Dashboard**

 📌 **Overview**
This project delivers an **executive dashboard** for the Medicare Health System, designed to provide actionable insights into hospital operations, patient demographics, departmental performance, and financial outcomes. It helps healthcare administrators and analysts make **data-driven decisions** to improve patient care and optimize resources.

---

🎯 **Problem Statement**
Hospitals face challenges such as:
- Rising **readmission rates** in critical departments (e.g., Oncology, Nephrology).
- Difficulty in tracking **mortality rates** and **length of stay** across branches.
- Lack of visibility into **insurance distribution** and its impact on revenue.
- Inefficient resource allocation due to poor monitoring of **bed occupancy**.
- Limited ability to identify **high-risk diseases** driving admissions.

This dashboard solves these problems by consolidating data into a **single interactive view**.

---

🔍 **Key Analyses**
1. **Admissions Trend (Monthly)**  
   - Tracks patient inflow over time to identify seasonal or operational patterns.

2. **Readmission Rate by Department**  
   - Highlights departments with high readmission risks (Oncology being the highest).  
   - Supports targeted interventions to reduce avoidable readmissions.

3. **Revenue by Hospital Branch**  
   - Compares revenue performance across Bengaluru, Chennai, Delhi, and Hyderabad.  
   - Helps management identify profitable branches and underperforming ones.

4. **Admissions by Age Group & Gender**  
   - Reveals demographic trends (e.g., higher admissions in 46–60 age group).  
   - Supports planning for age-specific healthcare programs.

5. **Top 10 Diseases by Admission**  
   - Identifies leading causes of hospitalization (Hypertension, Diabetes, Heart Disease).  
   - Enables preventive care strategies and resource prioritization.

6. **Insurance Distribution**  
   - Breaks down patient coverage: Government, Corporate, Premium, Self-pay.  
   - Assists in financial planning and negotiations with insurers.

7. **City-Level Performance Metrics**  
   - Provides granular insights into admissions, treatment costs, mortality, and revenue.  
   - Enables benchmarking across hospital branches.
